Selected Research & Projects

A curated selection of my most relevant work across efficient inference, robot manipulation, and scientific prediction.

Green-Edge-Cloud

An adaptive edge-cloud collaborative inference framework that dynamically routes queries between a lightweight edge Student model (Qwen-1.5B) and a cloud Teacher model (Qwen-72B) using training-free, zero-shot confidence scores.

Key contributions

  • Training-free adaptive routing via zero-shot confidence scores (reduces unnecessary cloud calls).
  • LoRA-based SFT on distilled CoT trajectories to improve logical reasoning and calibrate confidence distributions.
  • Real energy measurement with CodeCarbon, enabling a strong Pareto trade-off (81.16% energy reduction, 5.6x speedup).
81.16% energy reduction 5.6x speedup

Vision-Based Robot Manipulation

A 6-DOF teleoperation system integrating Action Chunking Transformer (ACT) and Conditional VAE (CVAE) to mitigate compounding errors, plus YOLOv5 for dynamic object segmentation.
